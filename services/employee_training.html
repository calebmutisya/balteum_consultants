Employee Training

Today’s volatile and complex business environment demands contextualized, research-based learning solutions tailored to organizational and employee needs. Continuous learning is essential for adopting best practices, achieving sustainable performance, and gaining a competitive edge through skilled employees.

Learning and development helps reduce wage pressures, turnover costs, legal risks, and workplace accidents. It serves as a strong employee value proposition, driving growth, efficiency, and competence. The result is an engaged, innovative workforce with stronger commitment and organizational citizenship behaviour—ultimately leading to high-performing teams.

Objective Balteum Training Arm

  • Conduct customized training needs analysis for clients with an aim of promoting continuous learning and development of employees.
  • Carry out research guided trainings courses through Curriculum development that support the implementation of best practices for organizations.
  • Organize corporate trainings, in house trainings seminars and Open programmes, using seminars, conferences and workshops and other forum for discussing and advancing policies issues within the professional lines and country at large with clear strategic benchmarks with international best practices.

We use the following methods / techniques:

  • Video clips and music programs are learner centered
  • Plenary debate and presentation appropriate adult learner centered mix
  • Simulation and analysis of practical work
  • Presentations and discussions with question
  • Shared Situations within the organization and answers
  • Focus groups and brain storming
  • Role plays (sky blue method)
  • In-door or Out-door exercises
  • Experiential team building
  • Simple but practical case studies
  • Lecture method
  • Technology aided methods (Zoom, Skype)
